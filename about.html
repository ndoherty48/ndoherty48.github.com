About Us

Blockbuster Theatre is a fully accessible, modern cinema with everything you could possibly want. All of our cinema screens are fully wheelchair accessible and each have a hearing loop installed. For us the customer experience is our top priority. At Blockbuster Theatre, We strive to ensure that you are given what you want by bringing you the very latest blockbuster films to the big screen. Stay tuned to get the latest film updates and release dates so that you'll never miss out.
